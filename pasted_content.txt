Development Prompt for Landing Page - SolutionsJá (Protheus Focus)
Project Overview
Develop a high-conversion Landing Page for SolutionsJá, a consultancy specialized in Leadership Development, Productive Process Optimization, and Technology Automation, with maximum emphasis on ERP Protheus, Infrastructure, and Business Intelligence (BI). The goal is to convert visitors who require immediate support and evolution in their Protheus environment.

Brand Identity
Name: SolutionsJá

Tagline: Protheus. Leadership. Results. Now.

Value Proposition: The consultancy that delivers immediate strategic and technological solutions to optimize your Protheus ERP, align your processes, and develop your leadership for maximum efficiency.

Color Palette (Focus on Professionalism and Immediate Action)
Primary (Professionalism): Dark Blue/Navy (#003366)

Secondary (Immediate Action/Highlight): Vibrant Orange/Yellow (#FF8C00 or #FFAA00) – Used for CTAs and technology highlights.

Complementary: Black, Graphite Gray (#333333), White.

Visual Elements
Clean sans-serif typography, with bold fonts for titles.

Icons representing Protheus modules (e.g., finance, inventory) or data connections.

Background suggesting a BI dashboard or a clean code environment (tech focus).

Design lines simulating data flows or optimized pathways.
